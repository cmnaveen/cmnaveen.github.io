The Earth's Bounty

We believe that true Svāda (taste) begins long before the kitchen. It starts in the soil. Our journey led us to passionate farmers across the country who nurture the land with generational wisdom and a deep reverence for nature.

“Every grain, every spice, every drop of oil has a story. By supporting honest farming, we bring you food that is full of life and flavor.”

Our Ingredients & Their Sources

We are proud to partner with farmers who share our commitment to purity and traditional practices.

🌾

Heritage Millets

Sourced directly from dryland farmers who cultivate drought-resistant, highly nutritious ancient grains. These millets are grown without synthetic chemicals, preserving their robust flavor and health benefits while supporting sustainable agriculture.

🍚

Traditional Rice

We choose heritage rice varieties from farmers who still use natural farming techniques in fertile river basins. These grains are minimally processed to retain their essential nutrients, bringing back the authentic, sweet aroma of true rice.

🌶️

Honest Spices

Our spices come from small-holder spice estates nestled in the hills. Hand-picked, sun-dried, and ground in small batches, our spices contain no fillers or artificial colors. They are vibrant, aromatic, and rich in essential oils.

🛢️

Cold-Pressed Oils

We use only traditional cold-pressed (Wood Churn / Kacchi Ghani) oils for all our cooking. Procured straight from artisans who crush seeds at low speeds to prevent heat generation, these oils retain their natural antioxidants and authentic flavor profiles.

Taste the Difference

Experience the pure flavors of these carefully sourced ingredients in our dishes.
