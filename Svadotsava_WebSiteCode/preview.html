The meaning behind our name

Svādotsava joins two beautiful ideas — taste and celebration.

Svāda

Svāda is not just taste on the tongue. It is the essence of flavor, the joy of relishing, and the memory a meal leaves behind.

Utsava

Utsava means celebration — a moment of joy, gathering, and upliftment. Together, Svādotsava becomes a celebration of taste in its deepest sense.

“At Svādotsava, every meal is meant to feel like an utsava — warm, memorable, and shared with joy.”

From coffee and tea to comforting food rooted in tradition, our restaurant is designed to be a place where people come together, slow down, and enjoy something meaningful.
