The Spirit of Svādotsava™

In the sacred Shastras, Svāda is more than taste—it is the soulful relishing of life’s essence. Combined with Utsava, which the Shiva Purana defines as a “celebration that removes worldly sorrow,” our name is our mission.

We believe that a meal should be a transformative experience—an offering of warmth, purity, and joy that leaves you lighter than when you arrived.

“At Svādotsava™, we don’t just serve food—we invite you to a celebration that removes the day’s weary through exceptional hospitality.”

☕ Specialty Coffee
Coffee

Espresso

Weigh 18g freshly ground House Blend (very fine grind). Tamp at 30lb, pull at 93.5°C/9 bar for 25–28 sec into a heated 70ml demitasse. Crema should be thick and tiger-striped. Serve immediately.

Signature
Coffee

Americano

Pull a double espresso (36g out from 18g) into a 180 ml cup. Slowly add 120 ml hot filtered water along the side of the cup — never pour water first. Stir gently once and serve black.

Classic
Coffee

Cappuccino

Pull a double espresso. Steam 120 ml whole milk: stretch for foam 3–4 sec, then rolling texture to 60–65°C. Pour in 3 stages — base pour, wiggle for heart shape, finish with a dusting of cocoa. Ratio: 1:1:1 espresso–milk–foam.

Classic
Coffee

Flat White

Pull a double espresso into a 160 ml cup. Steam 150 ml whole milk to 62°C with almost no foam — only micro-bubbles. Pour in one continuous motion from 3 cm height, creating a thin white layer on top. Slightly stronger coffee-to-milk ratio than a latte.

Bold
Coffee

Latte

Pull a double espresso. Steam 180 ml whole milk to 65°C with very fine microfoam. Pour in a steady stream while rocking the cup slightly to create a rosetta or heart latte art. Finish with a light cocoa dusting if desired.

Smooth
Coffee

Mocha

Add 25 ml of 70% dark chocolate ganache to the bottom of a 240 ml cup. Pull a double espresso directly on top and stir to melt the chocolate. Steam and pour 150 ml milk exactly like a latte. Top with cocoa or chocolate shavings.

Indulgent
Iced

Iced Americano

Fill a 340ml glass with ice. Add 80ml cold filtered water first to prevent dilution shock. Pull a double espresso and pour straight over the ice. Stir once and serve immediately.

Refreshing
Iced

Iced Latte

Fill a glass with 180g ice. Pour 180 ml cold whole milk (or alternative) over the ice. Pull a double espresso and pour slowly over the back of a spoon so it layers on top. Stir before drinking or serve with a straw.

Smooth
Iced

Cold Brew

Coarsely grind 100g Ethiopia Guji beans (like sea salt). Steep in 800 ml cold filtered water at 4°C for exactly 24 hours in a sealed container. Strain twice through a paper filter. Dilute 1:1 with water or milk when serving over ice.

Specialty
Iced

Nitro Cold Brew

Charge a 1 L nitro keg with the Cold Brew base at 2.5–3 bar of food-grade nitrogen. Fill a glass with ice and dispense slowly through the nitro faucet — a creamy head forms in 10 seconds. No milk added; nitrogen creates natural sweetness.

Premium
Singapore

Kopi Latte

Add 15ml condensed milk and 10ml gula melaka syrup to the cup. Pull a double espresso on top and stir vigorously to dissolve. Steam and pour 150ml whole milk like a latte. Finish with a sprinkle of finely ground kopi powder.

Singapore Style
Dessert

Gula Melaka Affogato

Drizzle 20 ml palm sugar (gula melaka + pandan) syrup inside a chilled coupe glass. Place 2 scoops vanilla gelato (or coconut for vegan) in the centre. Pour one fresh hot espresso shot over the gelato tableside for a dramatic melting effect. Serve with a spoon — eat immediately.
